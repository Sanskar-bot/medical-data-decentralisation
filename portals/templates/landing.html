Process

Three steps to sovereign health data

01
Register
Create your account. A unique RSA keypair is generated on your device. Your private key never leaves your machine — not even to us.
02
Encrypt
Your medical records are encrypted with AES-256-GCM using a data key that only you control. The ciphertext is stored; the key lives with you.
03
Control Access
When a doctor requests access, you approve it with your password. Access is time-limited to 24 hours and every event is audit-logged.

Architecture

Your data never leaves your hands

Records are encrypted before leaving your device
The server stores ciphertext only — never plaintext
Doctor access is cryptographically gated by your approval
Every access event is recorded in the immutable audit log
Keys are wrapped with PBKDF2 — your password, your key
